# 📊 Apocalypse Sales & Profit Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔍 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** analyzing sales performance, profitability, customer contribution, and time-based trends using a retail dataset.

The goal of this dashboard is to transform raw transactional data into **actionable business insights**.

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

* **Total Revenue:** 87.95K
* **Total Profit:** 43.21K
* **Units Sold:** 3K
* **Total Orders:** 74
* **Total Customers:** 4
* **Profit Margin:** 49.14%

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Product Performance Analysis

* Profit generated by each product
* Units sold per product
* Identification of high-profit vs high-volume products

---

### 🔹 Customer Contribution Analysis

* Profit contribution by each customer
* Matrix visualization of product vs customer performance
* Conditional formatting for better comparison

---

### 🔹 Time-Based Analysis

* Monthly contribution of units sold and total profit (%)
* Trend analysis showing performance decline over time

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* High sales volume does not always result in high profitability
* A few products (e.g., Weatherproof Jacket, Multitool Knife) contribute significantly to total profit
* Profit and unit contribution decline from January to March
* Customer contribution varies significantly across different products
